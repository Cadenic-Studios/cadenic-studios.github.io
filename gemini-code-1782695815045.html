DATA PROTECTION & PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 2026

Cadenic Studios operates the mobile application Delta-V. We are committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of our players.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application itself does not natively collect, log, or store any personal directly-identifiable human data. All local gameplay tracking, stardust progression, and currency balances are saved locally on your device via secure standard data storage.

2. Third-Party Services and Analytics

To support development, maintain stability, and provide monetization features, this Application utilizes trusted third-party SDKs including Google AdMob (for non-intrusive banner ads), RevenueCat (for secure transaction tracking), and Firebase (for anonymous performance telemetry and leaderboards).

3. Data Opt-Out Choices

Users can reset or limit their mobile device's tracking parameters at any time via their system-level Android or iOS device settings ("Opt out of Interest-Based Ads").
